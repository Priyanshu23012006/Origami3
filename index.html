Interesting Facts about Camel

  1. Camels are Domestic animals.
  2. Camels have thick lips which let them forage for thorny plants other animals can't eat.
  3. Camels have three sets of eyelids and two rows of eyelashes to keep sand out of their eyes.

Interesting Facts about Chameleon

  1. THEY MAINLY CHANGE COLOR IN ORDER TO COMMUNICATE OR REGULATE BODY TEMPERATURE.
  2. SKIN CRYSTALS ENABLE THEM TO CHANGE COLOR AT WILL
  3. THEIR FEET WORK LIKE SALAD TONGS

Interesting Facts about Pigeon

  1. Pigeons have fought alongside humans in World War I and II.
  2. Pigeons are the symbol of Peace.
  3. Pigeons are highly sociable animals. They will often be seen in flocks of 20-30 birds.

Interesting Facts about Teddy-bears

  1. Teddy-bears are usually soft.
  2. Teddy-bears are gifted to childrens on their birthday.
  3. Teddy-bears are cute.

Interesting Facts about Panda

  1. Pandas have so many fans because they look cute
  2. An adult panda can eat 12–38 kilos of bamboo per day!
  3. Giant pandas are good at climbing trees and can also swim.

Interesting Facts about Cicada

  1. Female Cicada may be attracted to the sound of motors.
  2. Cicadas can survive a huge fall as babies, or nymphs.
  3. The loud whirring or buzzing sound you hear is an all-male cicada chorus.
